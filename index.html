About

An engineer who chose purpose over profit.

Born and raised in Rajasthan, Pavan pursued engineering at the prestigious NIT Kurukshetra — one of India's premier technical institutions. But his ambitions always lay beyond the private sector.

Clearing competitive examinations, he joined the Government of India, bringing technical expertise into public service. Today he's based in Ranchi, Jharkhand — a city that represents his north-to-east journey across this vast nation.

Quiet, precise, and deliberate — he lets his work speak louder than words.
